    Smart Mobility Enabling Software Services

    Providing intelligent mobility applications through mobile app development and software solutions to enable smarter infrastructure for mobility in developing countries, and to empower integrated mobility globally. These services include E- Payment, Smart Parking, On- click Travel, Integrated Mobility, and more software- based tools that can be integrated in public, informal, and private mobility systems for optimized, personal and sustainable travel.

    Passenger- Centric Experience Design

    To build the experience architecture, we start by defining all design requirements, expected needs, behavior, and all possible elements to influence the mobility case. Later, all these variables are integrated in a solution model for digital visualization that can be used for simulation and analysis using our VR/ AR models. These data can be used for various customer- recipients evaluation.

    R&D for Disruptive Mobility

    To enable technology and development for disruptive mobility, access to development and testing is an essential need, yet, one of the biggest challenges. Our smart labs aim at empowering engineers, researchers, and students to conduct high end experimentation for disruptive mobility technologies. Our current Hyperoop lab provides access to development and testing for the Hyperloop and other transit systems technologies.

    • Your Innovation Hub for Disruptive Transportation Technologies

    Disruptive Mobility Technologies (DM TECH) started in 2018 to leverage disruptive technologies for sustainable mobility. DM Tech introduced the first patent-pending technology on Hyperloop Lab Facility for professional and educational training and experimentation in the area of hyperloop and related disruptive mobility systems and services. With the team great experience, the company also launched the first global platform that brings together different stakeholders involved in hyperloop technology. Moreover, DM TECH team provides state-of-the-art human-centred experience design for disruptive mobility systems and services. We also offer various advanced studies of feasibility, concept visualization, design analysis, and development of hyperloop and its subsystems.

    vision

    DM TECH aims at building an integrated community that can innovate and design its own sustainable mobility systems and services through facilitating access to knowledge, information and testing environments.

    To become the first R&D solution and service provider in the Middle-East and Africa for the Hyperloop technology and similar solutions.

    Mission

    We believe in creativity and diversity is our key success to an integrated solution and integrated community
